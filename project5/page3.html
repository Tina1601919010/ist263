HOW TO PLAY LOVE AND DEEP SPACE

Love and Deep Space merges intense sci-fi action with heartfelt romance, offering an experience where every decision matters.

MISSIONS & BATTLES

Take on thrilling missions as a Deep Space Hunter, battling mysterious "Wanderers" from the far reaches of the galaxy. Engage in real-time combat with stunning "Co-op Skills" alongside your companions, where seamless animations and dynamic interactions heighten the excitement.

RELATIONSHIP-BUILDING

Beyond combat, deepen your bonds with the male protagonists through immersive 3D interactions, daily companionships, and personalized storylines. Every choice influences the direction of your relationships, creating unique romantic arcs filled with emotional highs and lows.

CHARACTER CUSTOMIZATION

Express yourself with a high level of customization, including detailed avatar creation and personalized voice options, allowing you to truly inhabit your role in this universe.

TIPS FOR SUCCESS

  • Balance Combat and Romance: Engage in both thrilling battles and intimate moments to fully experience the game.
  • Explore Every Interaction: Unlock hidden dialogues and special scenes through thoughtful choices.
  • Master Your Skills: Enhance your combat abilities to face tougher challenges while strengthening bonds with your team.
