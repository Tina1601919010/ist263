WELCOME TO LOVE AND DEEP SPACE

Step into a near-future world where romance and danger collide. Love and Deep Space is a 3D interactive romance mobile game that blends deep emotional storytelling with thrilling sci-fi action. As a "Deep Space Hunter," you will uncover hidden truths, form bonds with unique characters, and face mysterious threats from the cosmos.

THE STORY

In a universe teetering on the edge of chaos, you are tasked with battling the enigmatic "Wanderers," mysterious lifeforms from the depths of space. Alongside compelling male protagonists, you’ll unravel the secrets behind these threats while navigating complex relationships that blur the line between duty and desire.

ARTISTIC STYLE

Featuring stunning real-time 3D rendering and a first-person perspective, Love and Deep Space delivers an immersive experience that places you at the heart of the story. The game’s visuals combine futuristic designs with emotional, character-driven moments, making every interaction feel personal and vivid.

BEGIN YOUR JOURNEY

Ready to explore the unknown and find love among the stars? Dive into Love and Deep Space and let your choices shape your destiny.
